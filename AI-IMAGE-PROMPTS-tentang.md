> Editorial industrial photography, premium B2B construction brand, monochrome palette (deep blacks, warm cream, raw concrete grey) with subtle royal cobalt blue accent (#1E40AF) only on details (workwear stripe, machinery panel, safety signage). Shot on Hasselblad / medium format look. Natural directional lighting, soft shadows, no harsh flash. Documentary feel, no stock-photo gloss. Indonesian context — local workers, tropical light, real construction sites. Slight film grain. NO text overlays, NO logos, NO watermarks, NO oversaturated colors.

## Tips eksekusi

- **Engine rekomendasi:** Midjourney v6 untuk style/cinematic, Flux Pro untuk realism + accurate hands, Ideogram kalau butuh teks (tapi semua prompt di atas sengaja minta NO TEXT).
- **Konsistensi karakter** (untuk team portraits): pakai `--cref` di Midjourney atau character LoRA di Flux supaya 3 team members terasa dari "perusahaan yang sama" — bisa pakai 1 foto referensi tone & lighting.
- **Color grade pas-final:** export semua hasil AI lalu apply same LUT di Lightroom — preset "industrial editorial" (lift blacks +5, drop saturation −15, warm shadows, cool highlights). Ini yang bikin 18 image terasa satu set.
- **Aspect ratio** di Midjourney pakai `--ar 21:9`, `--ar 4:5`, `--ar 3:4`, `--ar 1:1`, `--ar 16:10` sesuai list di atas.
